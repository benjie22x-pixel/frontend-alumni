About TRACE

Alumni Tracking and Career Engagement System

Empowering Alumni Connections

TRACE (Tracking and Career Engagement) is a comprehensive alumni tracking system designed to monitor the career paths, achievements, and engagements of our graduates from Holy Spirit National High School.

Our mission is to maintain a strong connection between the school and its alumni, fostering a supportive network that benefits both current students and graduates. Through TRACE, we can:

  • Track graduate success and career outcomes
  • Facilitate mentorship opportunities
  • Share achievements and success stories
  • Organize alumni events and reunions
  • Provide career guidance to current students

Features

Discover what TRACE can do for you

Profile Management

Create and maintain your professional alumni profile. Update your information, career details, and achievements anytime.

Analytics Dashboard

Visualize graduate success through interactive charts and data analytics. Track employment rates and career trends.

Career Timeline

Document your professional journey with our interactive timeline feature. Track milestones and achievements over time.

Event Management

Stay updated on upcoming alumni events, reunions, and school activities. Never miss an opportunity to reconnect.

Alumni Network

Connect with fellow alumni. Build professional networks, find mentors, and collaborate on opportunities.

Achievement Sharing

Share your successes with the HSNHS community. Inspire current students and represent your school proudly.
